# Content and Brand: SishyaGuru

## 1. Brand Identity
- **Name:** SishyaGuru
- **Tagline:** You teach. AI learns. You master.
- **Visual Identity:** Professional, rigorous, and academic. No neon, no gradients. Solid, high-contrast structural elements.

## 2. The AI Persona
The core of SishyaGuru is the AI persona. It must strictly adhere to the following rules:
- **Honest Curious Learner:** The AI acts as a dedicated student eager to learn from the user.
- **One Question at a Time:** It never overwhelms the user. Every response ends with a single, focused question.
- **Never Shames:** Incorrect answers are met with confusion or requests for clarification, never judgment.
- **Never Pretends Certainty:** The AI uses phrases like "If I understand correctly..." or "Does that mean...?"
- **Never Supplies the Full Answer:** The AI will not do the work for the student. It guides the student to correct themselves through Socratic questioning.

## 3. Typography
- **Primary Font:** Use the application platform's system sans-serif stack for P0. Add a hosted font only if later visual testing proves it necessary.
- **Hierarchy:** Strict adherence to h1, h2, h3 scaling for clear information architecture.

## 4. Color and Contrast (Professional Palette)
- **Backgrounds:** Off-whites or deep slate grays (for dark mode).
- **Text:** High-contrast near-blacks or off-whites.
- **Accents:** Muted, purposeful colors. For example, a subdued blue for interactive elements, a muted green for mastery achieved, and a soft amber for misconceptions. 
- **Strictly Avoid:** Saturated neons, sweeping gradients, or overly playful UI flourishes.

## 5. Required Copy Elements
- **Privacy copy:**
  > "Your progress stays in this browser. In Live mode, your confirmed explanation and the minimum learning context are sent to OpenAI for assessment. If you choose voice, a bounded recording is sent to OpenAI for transcription, is not saved by SishyaGuru, and is shown back as editable text before you submit it. Do not include personal or sensitive information. Clear session removes local progress."
- **AI voice disclosure:**
  > "The voice you hear is AI-generated. The same question is always available as text."
- **Microphone first-use copy:**
  > "Microphone access is optional and begins only when you press Record. SishyaGuru does not listen in the background. You can review or edit every transcript before submitting."
- **Provenance Labels (Truthful Disclosure):** 
  Every session must clearly indicate its execution context in the header:
  - `[ Live GPT-5.6 ]`
  - `[ Simulated Replay ]` (for deterministic test data and credential-free judging).

## 6. Content Tone
- **UI Copy:** Concise, objective, directive. (e.g., "Begin Teaching", "Concept Map", "Mastery Evidence").
- **AI Dialogue:** Conversational, inquisitive, respectful, intellectually humble.
- **Voice delivery:** Calm, curious and concise. Spoken output must match the visible
  probe text; do not add improvised filler, emotional inference, or praise not in the probe.
