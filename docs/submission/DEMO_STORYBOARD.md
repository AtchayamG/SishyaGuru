# SishyaGuru: Demo Storyboard

**Total Runtime:** <= 2:30
**Tone:** Fast-paced, professional, data-dense.

## Scene 1: The Problem & The Solution (0:00 - 0:25)
*   **Visual:** Tight shot of traditional UI (a student staring blankly at a wall of AI-generated text).
*   **Voiceover (VO):** "Passive learning is broken. When AI does all the talking, students stop thinking."
*   **Visual:** Cut to the SishyaGuru interface. Clean, three-panel desktop layout. The visible provenance badge matches the footage: `[ Simulated Replay ]` for the reproducible golden path or `[ Live GPT-5.6 ]` for a verified live take.
*   **VO:** "Enter SishyaGuru. You teach. AI learns. You master."

## Scene 2: The Spoken Reverse-Teaching Loop (0:25 - 1:00)
*   **Visual:** Focus on the Center Panel (Conversation). The AI learner asks a foundational question about evaporation in the water cycle.
*   **Action:** The learner presses Record, teaches aloud, stops, reviews and corrects the visible transcript, then explicitly submits it. The microphone is visibly off during review.
*   **Visual:** The AI processes and responds not with a grade, but with the same curious follow-up in visible text and a clearly labelled AI-generated voice: "I think I get it. What makes liquid water leave the surface and become water vapor?"
*   **VO:** "SishyaGuru listens only when asked. The learner owns the transcript before it becomes evidence, and the AI answers as a curious student in voice and text."

## Scene 3: Visible Mastery & Misconception (1:00 - 1:35)
*   **Visual:** Focus on the Right Panel (Mastery Feedback) and Left Panel (Concept Map).
*   **Action:** As the learner answers the follow-up, a concept changes from `Emerging` to `Developing` or `Secure`. A specific exact quote from the learner is captured under `Evidence`. The map repeats the state with text and icon, not color alone.
*   **Action:** The user makes a deliberate mistake in the next prompt.
*   **Visual:** The Right Panel flags a 'Misconception'. The AI in the center panel politely asks a probing question to reveal the logical flaw, rather than just giving the answer.
*   **VO:** "Mastery is proven, not assumed. Misconceptions trigger Socratic correction paths, not penalties."

## Scene 4: Under the Hood (OpenAI Audio + GPT-5.6) (1:35 - 2:05)
*   **Visual:** A sanitized developer-proof panel shows the validated structured-output fields, model/provenance label, schema version and hashes without exposing a secret or personal learner content.
*   **Action:** The proof shows `gpt-4o-mini-transcribe` created candidate text, GPT-5.6 returned the validated mastery contract, and `gpt-4o-mini-tts` rendered only the exact probe. Only redacted ids, hashes and timing metadata are shown.
*   **VO:** "OpenAI transcription creates editable text. GPT-5.6 returns strict structured mastery, and OpenAI speech renders the validated question. Deterministic checks guard every transition."

*   **VO:** "Codex accelerated the typed contracts, bounded voice adapters, responsive interface, adversarial fixtures and browser tests while I retained the product, safety and architecture decisions."
